üöÄ Project Overview

MyApply is an AI-powered resume tailoring platform that helps job seekers optimize their resumes for specific job postings. By leveraging OpenAI's language models and a structured experience graph, the platform intelligently matches your skills and achievements to job requirements while maintaining ATS (Applicant Tracking System) optimization.

Each job posting becomes a living workspace where you can:

  • See all tailoring attempts in one place with real-time status indicators
  • Track completion rates and identify which jobs need more work
  • View timeline of sessions to understand your iteration process
  • Quick-create new sessions directly from job cards with one click
  • Compare performance across multiple tailoring attempts per job

Experience Graph Flow

Your professional experience is stored as a graph structure in JSON format, containing:

  • Experiences: Work, leadership, projects, and activities with dates and context
  • Skills: Technical and soft skills mapped directly to each node
  • Achievements: Metrics, outcomes, and impact statements that feed bullet generation
  • Relationships: Connections the scorer uses when matching snippets to job buckets

Each tailoring run scores the graph, distills the top snippets into 40-word summaries, and keeps their achievements handy so the guardrails can verify every generated claim.

The tailoring service runs three concise OpenAI calls so every response stays grounded in the experience graph.

Resume Draft

Consumes job profile + snippet summaries and returns bullets with snippet IDs and stretch data.

Guardrail Audit

Rechecks each bullet against the source snippet and requested stretch level, flagging any overreach.

üèóÔ∏è System Architecture

Key components:

  • AgentKitTailoringService: builds job profiles, scores graph snippets, runs staged OpenAI calls, and applies guardrails.
  • Celery worker: `process_tailoring_session` locks the session, normalizes parameters, and persists every output field in one transaction.
  • Output metadata: bullet details, guardrail findings, layouts, talking points, and extracted job location coordinates live alongside the raw sections for easy review.
  • Token tracking: prompt/completion totals from each OpenAI call are merged so quotas, billing, and analytics stay accurate.
